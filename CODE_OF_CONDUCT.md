# 📜 Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a welcoming and inclusive environment for
everyone contributing to **Geo Sentinel AI**.

Participants are expected to treat each other with respect,
professionalism, and kindness.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Expected Behavior

-   Be respectful and inclusive.
-   Provide constructive feedback.
-   Focus on improving the project and helping the community.
-   Respect different viewpoints and experiences.
-   Use welcoming and inclusive language.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior will not be tolerated:

-   Harassment or discrimination
-   Personal attacks or insults
-   Trolling or disruptive comments
-   Publishing private information without permission

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct
and may remove or reject comments, commits, issues, or other
contributions that violate these guidelines.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it
through:

🌐 https://haybnz.web.app/#section-contact

All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Community Goal

Our goal is to build a collaborative environment for developing an
advanced **open geospatial intelligence platform**.

Thank you for helping make **Geo Sentinel AI** a respectful and
productive community. 🚀
